How to update:
From Main Android screen click Settings>Apps>SonusTv>clearData> Click home button on Remote
Open Sonus Tv Again and click "start" to download latest version
Once complete, click "ok" then "ok" again and click home button on your remote and restart Box by unplugging or turning off.
Open Sonus Tv and let sit a few min to let auto updates come through.
